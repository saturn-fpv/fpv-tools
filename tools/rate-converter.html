Rate Converter

Compare and convert rates across Actual, Betaflight, Quick, KISS, and RaceFlight models.

📖 Overview

Recreate the exact stick feel when moving between different flight controllers or firmware versions. This tool models rate curves mathematically to convert stick sensitivity and maximum deg/s rotation rates across different algorithms.

🛠️ How to Use

  1. Set your active rate values using the sliders or numerical inputs for Roll, Pitch, and Yaw.
  2. Choose your source rate system (e.g. Betaflight) and your target rate system (e.g. Actual or KISS) from the dropdown selectors.
  3. Click Convert to run a hybrid grid search algorithm that automatically finds the mathematically closest rates for your target firmware.

📊 Understanding the Charts

The interactive chart overlays your source curve against the target curve. The curve visualizes rotation speed (deg/s) across stick travel (0% center to 100% full deflection), allowing you to verify center stick sensitivity (RC Rate / Expo) alongside maximum spin rate.
